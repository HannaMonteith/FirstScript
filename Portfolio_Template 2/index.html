About Me

I love nature and maps. I love looking at problems spatially and mapping their impacts. I also make maps on my own time for fun! Down below I have the three places I've lived and some landmarks about them with maps.

Philadelphia

I grew up here and my family has lived in the city for multiple generations. It's known for it's cheesesteaks, the declaration of independence and it's crazy sports fans!

Burlington

I go to college here and have spent the past 3 years meeting the best people and learning a ton. It's known for it's waterfront of Lake Champlain, maple cremees and it's views of the iconic green mountians!

Copenhagen

I've spent my most recent summer here, living and studying while also embracing the amazing culture. It's known for it's picture perfect port, home to one of the biggest producers of beer and for having the happiest citizens!
